About Us

ABOUT US

We are the helping hand to balance the ecosystem. Since they can’t be stopped for creating unnecessary waste, here’s when we approach to clean up their works and providing solutions to it.We aim to restore marine life ,clean & maintain the coral reefs as into the main ecosystem.

VISION

We envision healthy reservoirs and oceans in which humanity is forever committed to carrying for and appreciating our ecosystem and our global resources and marine biodiversity for a long term benefit of people and all the life on Mother Earth.

MISSSION

Since we cannot stop creating waste and throwing it in open in rivers or marines, so we could atleast be the helping hand to clean it, recycle it, reuse it and create a balanced ecosystem and safe environment to live in.

Projects

PROJECT BRINE

Cleaning not only rivers but also seas and oceans which are second most important part of our ecosystem and restoring its life line which are corals in our project named “BRINE”.

PROJECT THIRST

Our project “THIRST” which is cleaning and using portable water from waste lands and ruins so that the next generation should not suffer due to our mistakes. We intend to achieve our project “THIRST” by a margin of 70 percent till 2022.

PROJECT GREEN

Removing the sludge and mud waste from the beds of rivers can be used back as organic fertilizers mixed with other organic and inorganic settlements which is our project “GREEN”.
